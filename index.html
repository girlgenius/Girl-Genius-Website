Our Philosophy and Impact

Girl Genius fosters an inclusive community where everyone is given a voice to empower the next generation of female leaders in STEAM. We are confident that anyone can pursue careers in STEAM and challenge conventional gender standards. We are proud to have taught 35,000+ girls with our STEM material and reached over 400,000 girls worldwide through our workshops and efforts on social media. With 40+ chapters and more coming, we are committed to driving even greater change in the future, helping shape a world where every girl can pursue her technical aspirations with confidence!
