Our Philosophy and Impact

Girl Genius fosters an inclusive community where everyone is given a voice to empower the next generation of female leaders in STEAM. We are confident that anyone can pursue careers in STEAM and challenge conventional gender standards. We are proud to have reached over 460,000 girls worldwide across 35+ countries through our efforts and remain committed to driving even greater change in the future.
